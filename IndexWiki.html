Relevant IT Legislation, Standards, and Compliance

Legislation/Regulation Details/Relevance Business Usage
Data Protection Act (2018) & GDPR Protects personal data of customers. Ensures secure data handling and compliance.
Freedom of Information Act (2000) Relevant for public sector requests. Ensures lawful and prompt responses to FOI requests.
Equality Act (2010) Ensures accessibility for all, including disabilities. Websites and apps adhere to WCAG accessibility standards.
ISO/IEC 27001 Establishes information security management systems (ISMS). Protects data, reduces risks, and enhances trust.
ISO 22301 Standard for business continuity and resilience. Ensures operations during disruptions (e.g., IT failures).
